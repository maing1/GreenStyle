The Atelier

A wardrobe that grows old with you.

GreenStyle was founded in Lisbon, Portugal by four designers who shared one belief: fashion should respect both people and the planet.

We work in small batches of fifty. Each garment passes through six pairs of hands — from the fiber growers in the Alentejo plains to the menders who repair pieces sent back for a second life.

Our promises

  • Lifetime free repair on every garment
  • Take-back program with 20% credit toward your next piece
  • Shipped in seed paper packaging — plant it after
  • Carbon-neutral by design, not offset
